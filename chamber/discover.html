Quick History

Founded in 1781 as an agricultural pueblo, Los Angeles quickly became a global economic powerhouse. A late 19th-century railroad boom drove rapid expansion, sparking the creation of our Chamber of Commerce in 1888. Over the next century, the region led major breakthroughs in aviation, trade, and cinema. Today, Los Angeles thrives as a dynamic hub of culture, technology, and diverse enterprise.
